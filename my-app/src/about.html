About Us

UniTrade is a platform designed to facilitate buying, trading, and selling activities among students of Caraga State University. Our goal is to create a seamless and convenient experience for students to connect with each other and engage in various transactions, including buying and selling items, trading goods, and sharing resources.

UniTrade

Whether you're looking to purchase textbooks for your next semester, trade your old gadgets for something new, or sell items you no longer need, UniTrade provides a user-friendly platform where you can easily list, browse, and interact with listings from fellow students.

Join UniTrade today and become part of our growing community of students who are leveraging the platform to save money, find great deals, and connect with peers in the Caraga State University community.
